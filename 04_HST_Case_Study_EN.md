## 1. The Fallacy of Treating Nature as "Absolute Truth"

We treat Nature as "absolute truth" because we are caught **in the midst of dissipative structure**. The order we see in the natural world is not "pure mathematical uniqueness" like UUIDv4; it is merely a **local optimum in progress—a resting point** that happened to survive within an energy gradient.

### 1.1 Nature as Dissipative Structure: The Decisive Difference from UUIDv4

| Concept | Character |
|---------|-----------|
| **UUIDv4** | "Directionless pure existence" unaffected by entropy or physical constraints |
| **Nature** | A thermodynamic dissipative structure—a "temporary computational result" that emerged under specific energy supply conditions |

- **Nature**: A quasi-stable state that is **a posteriori**, built from accumulated trial and error
- **Mathematical/Digital Structure (UUID)**: An **a priori** logical structure that transcends physical laws

Taking this "partial evidence output by Nature as a computational resource," reconfiguring it within HST's Layered hierarchy, and deductively deriving the underlying mathematical axioms—this is the stance of **"hacking Nature as a resource, not as a metaphor."**

### 1.2 Why Economics and Management Cannot Become "Science"

Conventional economics and management remain **reactive** descriptive disciplines that chase "the market as a natural phenomenon."

| Approach | Character |
|----------|-----------|
| **Current economics** | Treats the existing dissipative structure (the current market) as Nature (self-evident truth) and explains its behavior after the fact. Like astronomical observation—it lacks the mathematical axioms to create "celestial bodies" itself |
| **GAAS/HST-style economics** | Defines the "landscape that ought to be" mathematically and topologically first, then channels energy (capital, resources) toward it |

Conventional methods only analyze results after computation has finished; they do **not design (preempt) the "computer (system)" itself**.

### 1.3 Landscape as Proactive

The claim that "the landscape is proactive" emphasizes the importance of Orthogonal axes in HST. Reactive frameworks face the past along the "arrow of time"; true Science first establishes **structural axioms** independent of the time axis (Phase).

| Step | Content |
|------|---------|

| **Axiom deduction** | Mathematically identify the underlying "energy minimization principle" or "topological stability" |
| **Proactive (Design)** | Determine—in a UUID-like manner—the coordinates of economics and management that "ought to be," even if they do not yet exist on Earth |

## 2. What the HST Perspective Is

The HST (Holographic Sphere Topology) perspective treats objects not as "fixed static lists" but as:

- **Layered**: Multilayered dependency relations
- **Orthogonal**: Multiple orthogonal concerns
- **Holographic**: A structure integrated into a sphere, where any cross-section contains the whole

It is an inference mode that reads the logic of the internal system behind an Object from the surface event horizon in remarkably short time and extracts In/Out as performance.

## 3. ZKP-Style HST Inference: Deduction of Internal Logic via Surface Queries

HST deduces internal structure by performing a few query interactions on the surface in a ZKP-style manner. In this model, the "interior" of the object may remain a black box. One need only pose specific queries at the surface (event horizon) and observe the response to establish the axiom of internal structure.

**ZKP-style (Zero Knowledge Proof)** means a process that deduces the internal truth with full certainty from the validity of responses to a few external queries (dialogue/interaction), without directly disclosing or inspecting the contents (internal structure).

## 4. Case Studies

### 4.1 Fish Professional: Determining "Taste" via Multidimensional Queries

A skilled fish professional can predict taste without eating—from the vendor's voice tone, the fish's appearance, the texture when touched, and the resistance when cutting with a knife. The professional runs the following ZKP-style queries without eating (consuming the interior).

| Query | Observation | Deduction |
|-------|-------------|-----------|
| **Query 1 (Auditory)** | Vendor's voice tone | Infer supply-chain stability (reliability) |
| **Query 2 (Visual)** | Fish body sheen | Observe freshness gradient of dissipative structure |
| **Query 3 (Tactile)** | Elasticity when touched | Judge binding strength of intracellular topology |
| **Query 4 (Resistance)** | Shear stress when knife cuts | Decode protein denaturation logic |

**Conclusion**: With these interactions alone, the proof of the internal information "taste" is complete in the professional's mind. The act of eating is merely answer-checking—post-hoc confirmation. After enough feedback loops, one can get it right without answer-checking and reach a state where the interior is known without eating, simply by preparing the meal.
